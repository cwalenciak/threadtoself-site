Help & Support

Thread to Self is intentionally small. Most questions should have simple answers.

Frequently asked questions

1. Where are my messages stored?
Messages are stored on the iPhone and synchronized through the user's private iCloud account when available.
2. Does Thread to Self require an account?
No.
3. Can the developer read my messages?
No. The developer does not operate a database containing users' message content.
4. Can I edit a message?
No. Messages can only be permanently deleted.
5. Can I recover a deleted message?
No. Deletion is permanent.
6. What happens after the 30-day trial?
Writing becomes locked. Existing messages remain readable and exportable, and deletion, Jump, Settings, local storage, and iCloud synchronization continue to work.
7. How much does the app cost?
$4.99 as a one-time purchase after the trial.
8. How do I restore a purchase?
Open Settings in Thread to Self and tap Restore Purchase while signed into the same Apple Account used for the purchase.
9. How do I export my thread?
Open Settings and tap Export Thread.
10. Why is iCloud synchronization unavailable?
The user may be signed out of iCloud, have restricted iCloud access, have no network connection, or Apple's services may be temporarily unavailable.
