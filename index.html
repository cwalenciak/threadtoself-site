Thread to Self

Capture a thought and move on.

Thread to Self is one private message thread with yourself. No clutter, no sorting system, and no social noise.

Product principles

  • One continuous stream
  • No files or folders
  • No tags
  • No search or filters
  • No editing after a message is added
  • Messages can only be permanently deleted
  • No photos or attachments
  • No AI
  • No social features
  • No ads

Availability and pricing

Thread to Self is available for iPhone.

You can write freely during a 30-day trial. To keep writing after the trial, purchase a one-time unlock for $4.99.

After the trial ends, existing messages remain readable and exportable. Messages are stored on your iPhone and synchronized through your private iCloud account when iCloud is available.
