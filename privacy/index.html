Thread to Self

Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 21, 2026

Overview

This policy explains how Thread to Self handles information. Thread to Self is made by You-R-Here LLC.

Thread to Self does not require an account. You-R-Here LLC does not operate a user database for Thread to Self.

The developer does not collect, sell, rent, or share users' message content.

How information is handled

  • The app does not contain advertising or third-party analytics.
  • Messages are stored locally on the user's device using Apple technologies.
  • Messages may synchronize through the user's private Apple iCloud account using CloudKit.
  • The developer cannot read or recover a user's private messages.
  • Users may export their thread as a text file.
  • Users may permanently delete individual messages.
  • Deleting the app may not immediately delete information synchronized through the user's private iCloud account.

Apple services and purchases

Purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store.

Apple may process purchase, device, diagnostic, and iCloud information under Apple's own policies.

Children, updates, and contact

The app is not designed to collect sensitive information from children.

This policy may be updated over time. When it changes, the effective date above will be updated.
